Welcome to El Taco Rey, a restaurant for an authentic Mexican street food experience. We value the authentic experience because we feel that our city is lacking any meaningful Mexican streeet food experience. Therefore we strive to bring that to our city and at the same time seperate ourselves from our competition. When you leave our restaurant our goal is that you will feel like you are stepping into a whole new world.

We have been brining an authentic Mexican street food exprience for over 20 years now. We hope to continue to do for many more years to come. We are sure that once you come and have a taste of our delicios tacos, or bite into your first authentic torta, that you will wan to keep coming back. Through our food we hope to bring together our city and its people through a shared unique experience, so come and enjoy authentic Mexican street food!
